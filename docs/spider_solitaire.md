## Rules of Spider Solitaire

### ğŸƒ Objective
The goal is to arrange all the cards in descending sequences from **King to Ace** of the same suit within the tableau. Once a complete sequence is formed, it is automatically removed from the tableau. The game is won when all eight sequences are completed.
